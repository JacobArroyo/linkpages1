My Favorite Shows

Comedy & Sitcoms

The Big Bang Theory

CBS | 12 Seasons | Sitcom

Mensa-level physicists Leonard and Sheldon understand everything from the quantum vacuum to dark matter, but they are completely lost when it comes to people.

Young Sheldon

CBS | 7 Seasons | Coming-of-Age Comedy

A prequel to The Big Bang Theory, following 9-year-old Sheldon Cooper as he skips four grades to start high school alongside his less-intellectual older brother.

Brooklyn Nine-Nine

NBC | 8 Seasons | Police Comedy

Detective Jake Peralta is a great cop, but he's never had to follow the rules too closely. That changes when a new, strict captain joins the 99th precinct.

The Office (US)

NBC | 9 Seasons | Mockumentary Sitcom

A documentary crew follows the daily lives of office employees at the Dunder Mifflin Paper Company, led by their deluded but well-meaning manager, Michael Scott.

Dexter

Showtime | 8 Seasons | Crime Drama

He's smart, he's good looking, and he's got a great sense of humor. He's Dexter Morgan, everyone's favorite serial killer who lives by a strict code.
