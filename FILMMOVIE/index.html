Movie Collection

My Favorite Films

Action

Gladiator

Dir: Ridley Scott | Stars: Russell Crowe

A former Roman General sets out to exact vengeance against a corrupt emperor.

John Wick

Dir: Chad Stahelski | Stars: Keanu Reeves

An ex-hit-man comes out of retirement to track down the gangsters who took everything from him.

Adventure & Discovery

The Mummy

Dir: Stephen Sommers | Stars: Brendan Fraser

An American serving in the French Foreign Legion on an archaeological dig at the ancient city of Hamunaptra accidentally awakens a mummy.

The Mummy Returns

Dir: Stephen Sommers | Stars: Brendan Fraser

Rick and Evelyn O'Connell are back in action when the Scorpion King returns to life and threatens to conquer the world.

The Mummy: Tomb of the Dragon Emperor

Dir: Rob Cohen | Stars: Brendan Fraser

In 1946, archaeologist Rick O'Connell and his son Alex are called upon to stop the evil Chinese Emperor Han from using an army of undead warriors to conquer the world.

Raiders of the Lost Ark

Dir: Steven Spielberg | Stars: Harrison Ford

Archaeologist and adventurer Indiana Jones is hired by the U.S. government to find the Ark of the Covenant before the Nazis can get their hands on it.

Temple of Doom

Dir: Steven Spielberg | Stars: Harrison Ford

In 1935, Indiana Jones arrives in India and discovers a secret society of occultists who are using a powerful artifact to control the local population.

The Last Crusade

Dir: Steven Spielberg | Stars: Harrison Ford

In 1938, after his father Professor Henry Jones, Sr. goes missing while pursuing the Holy Grail, Indiana Jones finds himself in a race against the Nazis to find the Grail before they can use it for their own evil purposes.

Kingdom of the Crystal Skull

Dir: Steven Spielberg | Stars: Harrison Ford

In 1957, after being abducted by Soviet agents, Indiana Jones is rescued by his old friend Mac and taken to a secret government warehouse where he learns that the Soviets are searching for a telepathic crystal skull that is believed to be of extraterrestrial origin.

The Dial of Destiny

Dir: James Mangold | Stars: Harrison Ford

In 1969, after being abducted by Soviet agents, Indiana Jones is rescued by his old friend Mac and taken to a secret government warehouse where he learns that the Soviets are searching for a telepathic crystal skull that is believed to be of extraterrestrial origin.

Sci-Fi

Iron Man

Dir: Jon Favreau | Stars: Robert Downey Jr.

After being held captive in an Afghan cave, billionaire engineer Tony Stark creates a unique weaponized suit of armor to fight evil.

Iron Man 2

Dir: Jon Favreau | Stars: Robert Downey Jr.

With the world now aware of his identity as Iron Man, Tony Stark must contend with both his declining health and a vengeful madman with ties to his father's legacy.

Iron Man 3

Dir: Shane Black | Stars: Robert Downey Jr.

When Tony Stark's world is torn apart by a formidable enemy, he must rely on his ingenuity and the help of his closest allies to survive.
