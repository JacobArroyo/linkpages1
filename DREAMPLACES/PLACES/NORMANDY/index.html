Normandy, France

Why I Want to Visit Normandy

Historic Landscape & Medieval Wonder

"Normandy, France, is on my dream list primarily because I am fascinated by the blend of medieval history and 20th-century events. The region feels both quaint and epic, and I long to witness its power in person."

I want to walk the ancient, narrow streets of **Mont Saint-Michel**, a true wonder that looks as if it's straight from a fairy tale.

I also dream of visiting the **D-Day Landing Beaches** to reflect on the immense bravery and sacrifice that took place on these historic shores.
