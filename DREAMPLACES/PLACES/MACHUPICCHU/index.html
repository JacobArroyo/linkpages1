Places I long to explore and experience.

Cusco, Peru

Why I Want to Visit Machu Picchu

The Lost City of the Incas

"Machu Picchu is a destination that feels otherworldly. I am drawn to the mystery of how this city was built atop a mountain ridge and the spiritual energy that many travelers describe when standing amidst the ancient stone structures."

I want to see the sun rise over the **Intihuatana stone**, witnessing the incredible precision of Incan engineering and astronomy.

I dream of hiking a portion of the **Inca Trail**, experiencing the breathtaking Andean scenery and the lush cloud forests that lead to the citadel.
