Shinjuku, Tokyo

Why I Want to Visit Kamurocho

The City That Never Sleeps

"Kamurocho represents the heart of urban nightlife and drama. Whether it's the neon-lit archway of Tenkaichi Street or the hidden alleys of Champion District, I am fascinated by the stories told through its dense, vibrant architecture."

I want to walk under the iconic **Tenkaichi Street Gate**, feeling the buzz of the city and the glow of the red neon signs.

I dream of standing at the base of the **Shinjuku Toho Building**, the center of all the major events and conflicts that define the district's history.
